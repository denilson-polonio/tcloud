Telegram bot
How do I create a Telegram bot?
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather (the official one, with the blue check).
  2. Send /newbot, choose a name and a username ending in “bot”.
  3. BotFather replies with a token like 123456:ABC… — paste it below. Keep it secret.

Your bot starts immediately — then you can send it /id (in a chat, or post /id inside your channel) to get the IDs you need next.

Storage channel
How do I create the channel and find its ID?
  1. In Telegram create a new private channel (e.g. “My TCloud Storage”). This is where your files will live.
  2. Add your bot to the channel as administrator (it needs to post, edit and delete messages).
  3. Post any message in the channel, then forward it to your bot in a private chat: the bot replies with the channel ID (starts with -100…).
  4. Alternative: send /id inside the channel — the bot answers with the ID.
