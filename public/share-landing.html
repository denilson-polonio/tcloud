Your private cloud, powered by Telegram.

TCloud is a self-hosted cloud that turns Telegram into unlimited, end-to-end encrypted storage for your files. Upload from the web, the desktop, or straight from a Telegram bot — then share anything with a private or public link.

Unlimited storage

Encrypted & private

Every chunk is encrypted before it leaves your server. You hold the keys.

Share your way
